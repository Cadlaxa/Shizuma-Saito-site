Shizuma Saito

靜間彩冬

Shizuma Saito (靜間彩冬) is a trilingual UTAU voicebank that supports English, Filipino and Japanese. He has a quadpitch CVVC and Arpasing configuration, which are technical terms for the way his voicebank is organized to allow for more natural and flexible singing.


  • - 靜間 (Shizuma):

    This means "quiet" or "calm space."

  • - 彩冬 (Saito):

    This translates to "colorful" or "vivid winter."
